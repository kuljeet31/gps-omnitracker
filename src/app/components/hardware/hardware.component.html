Integrated devices

Wialon vehicle tracking system is universal. It’s currently integrated with more than 2,000 models of GPS tracking devices, including personal GPS trackers, automobile controllers and software-based "trackers" installed as mobile apps on smartphones. Additionally, Wialon is able to process data, received from after-market sensors (weight, humidity, light, temperature and others), cameras, tachograph and other supplementary GPS hardware, connected to GPS tracking devices.
